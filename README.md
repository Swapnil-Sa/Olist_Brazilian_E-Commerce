# 📊 Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes the Brazilian e-commerce dataset (Olist) to uncover key business insights related to **sales performance, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction**.

The analysis focuses on identifying:
- Revenue trends
- Top-performing categories and regions
- Delivery performance and its impact on customer ratings
- Business growth opportunities

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales and revenue distribution
- Identify high-performing states and categories
- Evaluate delivery efficiency
- Measure the impact of delays on customer satisfaction
- Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Python** (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)
- **Excel** (Initial Data Handling)
- **Power BI** (Dashboard & Visualization)
- **DAX** (KPI Calculations)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🟡 Revenue Analysis
- Total Revenue: **~R$16M**
- São Paulo (SP) contributes **~37% of total revenue (~R$6M)**

---

### 🚚 Delivery Performance
- On-time delivery rate: **~92%**
- Late delivery rate: **~8%**
- Average delivery time: **~12 days**

---

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction
- Late deliveries reduce customer ratings by **~1.65 points**
- Delivery performance directly impacts customer experience

---

### 🌍 Regional Insights
- SP has:
  - Highest revenue
  - Fastest delivery (~8 days)
- Northern regions show:
  - Higher delivery delays
  - Lower revenue contribution

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1️⃣ Revenue Protection (RJ)
- RJ generates ~R$2.1M but has high delay rates (~13%)
- Improving delivery can protect revenue and improve ratings

---

### 2️⃣ Growth Opportunity
- Health & Beauty is the top-performing category
- Expanding this segment can drive higher revenue

---

### 3️⃣ Seasonal Strategy
- Peak sales occur between **June–August**
- Businesses should increase inventory and marketing before peak season
